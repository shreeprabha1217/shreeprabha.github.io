FOODX, The Application to Reduce Food Wastage

FOODX, is an application developed with the concern of reducing food wastage. Application contains three modules. Initially the person who is interested in donating the extra food to the needy uploads food image along with relevant information like time of preparation, ingredients, type and place of preparation. Needy can log into the system check the shelflife of food based on the result. ID3 Algorithm is used to check the shelflife based on user inputs.

Technologies Used: ReactJS, ID3 Algorithm, XAMPP

Pharmacy Management System

Pharmacy management system is a mini application that is built to support small scale oharmacies to maintain record of their ongoing activities. System can be used to track the details of drugs brought into the pharmacy to prepare the medicines. Medicines thatwere sold out and the current stock left in the pharmacy.

Technologies Used: PHP, HTML, CSS, MySQL, XAMPP
